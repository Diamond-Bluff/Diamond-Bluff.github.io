Interest Form

Please fill out this form if you are interested in joining the Open Programmable Infrastructure Project. Once you have filled out the participation form you will be added to the contact sheet and have access to edit it. You can then join any desired subgroup by adding an x to the appropriate box. Also, it will help to email the group leader to make sure you are included in invites.
